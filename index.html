About the Doctor

Compassionate Care, Evidence-Based Rehabilitation

Dr. Abhay Santosh Sharma is a dedicated physiotherapist with a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPTh) from Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (MUHS). With a deep commitment to patient-centered rehabilitation, he brings advanced clinical skills and genuine compassion to every treatment session.

His approach integrates the latest evidence-based techniques with personalized care, ensuring each patient receives a tailored rehabilitation plan that addresses their unique needs — whether recovering from surgery, sports injuries, or navigating pregnancy.
