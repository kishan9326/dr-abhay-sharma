About the Doctor

Compassionate Care, Evidence-Based Rehabilitation

Dr. Abhay Santosh Sharma is a dedicated physiotherapist at Popular Health Checkup Centre with a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPTh) from Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (MUHS). With a deep commitment to patient-centered rehabilitation, he brings advanced clinical skills and genuine compassion to every treatment session — including home visits where needed.

His approach integrates the latest evidence-based techniques with personalized care — whether recovering from surgery, sports injuries, stroke, spinal cord injury, or navigating the journey of pregnancy and motherhood.
